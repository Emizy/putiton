How to post an advert on Putiton.com.ng

Posting Products on Putiton.com.ng is easy and fast, just take the following steps to proceed:

1. Click on Enroll as Supplier to Register For FREE NOW.

2. Complete all the information about your brand and Click Register Button.

3. After Registration successfull, then continue to login in order to access your dashboard.

4. Once you are on your dashboard click on Submits Ads to post your products and you will be require to submit three picture of your products.

5. Your advert will be published shortly once moderation process is completed.

Be ready to receive numerous incoming calls ,WhatsApp message(customized by you) and Emails from your potential buyers. Good luck with your sales!

Posting rules

Following rules are required while you are posting adverts on PutItOn:

1. Every advert must have a precise title.

2. Every advert should contain only unique images (taken by the seller and not downloaded from the Internet) without any contact information or watermarks.

3. Make sure you chose an appropriate category. Be attentive choosing a category for your products.

4. The prices of your items must correspond to the real prices of similar products.

5. All items and products must be legally permitted.

6. Each item for sale must be posted separately. You cannot post several products within one and the same advert.

7. Every advert must contain a brief and clear description.
